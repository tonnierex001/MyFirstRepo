My Personal and Academic Goals for Next Year

On a personal level, I want to improve my problem-solving skills, strengthen my discipline and consistency, and continue developing leadership abilities by working with and mentoring others in my community.

Overall, my focus is to keep learning, keep building, and keep growing into someone capable of creating meaningful change.
