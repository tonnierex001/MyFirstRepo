Brief Introduction About Myself

I am Anthony Imbufe, a purpose-driven tech enthusiast from Cross River State, Nigeria, passionate about building solutions that create real impact in underserved communities. With a strong foundation in Microsoft Office tools and a growing skill set in web development, I am currently on a journey to become a world-class engineer.

Through my work with RuralConnect, I focus on leveraging technology to improve healthcare access and empower young people with digital skills in rural areas. I believe that true innovation starts with understanding people, and I am committed to creating solutions for Africans, by Africans.

Driven by curiosity, leadership, and a desire to make a difference, I continue to learn, grow, and build toward a future where technology transforms lives across Africa.
